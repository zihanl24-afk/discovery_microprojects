8,000 metres

[edit]

There are 14 mountains over 8,000 metres (26,247 ft), which are often referred to as the eight-thousanders. (There are six more 8,000m peaks in Nepal, waiting for official recognition, making for a total of 20.[1]) All are in the two highest mountain ranges in the world, the Himalayas and the Karakoram.
